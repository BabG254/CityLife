Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming one of Kenya's most trusted real estate firms

2012

The Beginning

CityLife Real Estate was founded in August 2012 by a group of visionary professionals who recognized the growing potential in Kenya's real estate market. Starting with a small office in Nairobi, we began our journey with a simple mission: to provide honest, dedicated, and perfect service to every client.

2015

Growing Reach

By 2015, we had expanded our operations beyond Nairobi to include Kiambu, Thika, and other key areas. Our portfolio grew to include both residential and commercial properties, establishing us as a comprehensive real estate solution provider.

2018

Digital Innovation

Embracing technology, we launched our digital platform and introduced innovative property management systems. This technological advancement improved our efficiency and enhanced the client experience significantly.

2025

Market Leadership

Today, CityLife Real Estate stands as one of Kenya's premier real estate firms, managing over 500 properties and serving more than 1,200 satisfied clients. Our commitment to excellence continues to drive our success.
